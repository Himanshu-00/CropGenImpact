What We Offer?

Crop Monitoring

Enhanced Crop Health Monitoring

CropGen leverages indices like the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) and the Enhanced Vegetation Index (EVI) to assess plant health and vigor by measuring the density and health of plant canopies.

Optimized Resource Allocation

The Soil Adjusted Vegetation Index (SAVI) and the Modified Soil Adjusted Vegetation Index (MSAVI) adjust for soil brightness, offering accurate insights even in high soil reflection areas. This precision is crucial for optimizing irrigation and fertilization.

Precision Irrigation & Drought Management

The Normalized Difference Water Index (NDWI) and the Normalized Difference Moisture Index (NDMI) are critical in assessing soil moisture and irrigation needs, crucial for water conservation and drought management.

Advanced Agricultural Analytics

CropGen integrates these vegetation indices with AI algorithms to predict future crop health and yield trends. This predictive capability is vital for planning and resource allocation.

Sustainable Agricultural Practices

By providing detailed visibility into crop status across vast and varied landscapes, CropGen supports sustainable farming initiatives, which aim to reduce environmental impact while maintaining high productivity.

Scalability and Accessibility

CropGen’s cloud-based platform enables easy access to vegetation index data and insights from anywhere in the world. This scalability ensures that smallholder farmers and large agribusinesses alike can benefit from precision agriculture.
